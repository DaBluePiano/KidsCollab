<center>**Celestial**

<br></br>

The moon

Consoles tears

Illuminates

The gentle night sky

<br></br>

Stars

Guide you through

life and

Pinpoints joyful moments

<br></br>

The sun

A constant

Shines to provide

light for you and me

<br></br>

Humans

Breaths

Hate

Love</center>
